Product: Operations Feed + Holding Mode + Multibanking
[web, mobile]

operations feed — the product on the Alfa Bank homepage, showing all user activity: incoming and outgoing transactions, bank messages, payment registries, fees, and scheduled payments. it’s the most frequently used part of the platform and represents the bank’s core user experience. what i’ve done and what i’m doing now:
led a full redesign of the transaction table: improved readability of large data sets, enhanced filtering and designed two display modes for flexible data handling
presented and defended the solution to the DS team, CFO, and key stakeholders
conducted in-depth interviews with users and enhanced some solutions, then ran front-end reviews before and after release
my product has been rolled out to 70% of all business clients
currently finalizing user flows for integrating multibanking into my product.

the main challenge: creating a unified space where users can manage operations across up to 70 companies (holding mode) and across multiple banks — seamlessly and without overload.

Lead designer role — lead and mentor a team of 3 designers.
contribute to the internal design community: improve shared processes, run critiques, and help researchers, writers, and designers work better together.
